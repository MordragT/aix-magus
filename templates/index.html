About

Aix Magus is a german E-Sports Clan based in North-Rhine-Westphalia.

Aix Magus stands as a prominent force within the realm of German E-Sports, with its roots firmly planted in the vibrant heartland of North-Rhine-Westphalia. Pioneering a new era of competitive gaming, our clan has made its mark across a diverse array of gaming landscapes.

Founded in the year 2023, Aix Magus swiftly established itself as a powerhouse, assembling dedicated teams that showcase their prowess in an impressive array of games. Among our ranks, two standouts take center stage: the exhilarating battlefields of Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) and the strategic mastery of League of Legends (LoL).

Our journey through the digital arena is fueled by passion, dedication, and an unyielding commitment to excellence. With each click of the mouse and every perfectly timed maneuver, we unite as a collective force, propelling Aix Magus to the forefront of competitive E-Sports. As we continue to carve our path, our clan represents not only a community of players but a symbol of unwavering camaraderie and a shared pursuit of victory.

Join us on this exhilarating journey, as we redefine what it means to be part of an esports clan, and together, we aim to etch our names in the annals of gaming history.
